--- layout: post title: Beer Can Golf tags: - beer - cigarettes - golf - recreation - whimsy status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '1' --- Beer can golf - greatest thing ever. Grab yourself a smoke, some beers, some empties and a blunt object and have yourself a time. 1. Place cigar/cigarette in mouth, ignite. 2. Open yourself the first of your beers and begin consumption. 3. Line up a few empties at a time. 4. Swing away. 5. Repeat ad infinitum or until you run out of time.
