--- layout: post title: Fix Warnings tags: - miscellany status: draft type: post published: false meta: _edit_last: '2' --- I would ask of anyone writing a library these days to get rid of compile warnings. I find it a little disconcerting to see a library compiling with typecasting warnings between pointers. I won't name names here, but it is kind of unprofessional. So please, compile with -Wall and fix your warnings.
